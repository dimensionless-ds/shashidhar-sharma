About the Author
Shashidhar Sharma has spent more than three decades working at the intersection of organisational leadership, workplace strategy, and the future of knowledge work. His writing and advisory work spans five generations of professionals and dozens of industries across geographies. Work and Workplace at the Edge of Intelligence is the first book in The Edge of Intelligence series, followed by How to Flourish in the Age of AI and Panic to Power.
Shashidhar Sharma's Work and Workplace at the Edge of Intelligence – a groundbreaking new book that reframes the conversation on AI, productivity, and the human mind in the workplace.
At a moment when artificial intelligence is reshaping every industry, organisation, and career, this book makes a case that is both urgent and counterintuitive: the future of work does not depend on machines alone. It depends on our ability to understand – and protect – the distinctly human intelligence that no algorithm can replicate.

About the Book
Work and Workplace at the Edge of Intelligence explores why the future of workplace productivity sits at the intersection of three forces that most organisations are still treating as separate conversations: Artificial Intelligence, Climate Change, and the Human Mind. Drawing on decades of working with leaders, teams, and organisations across industries, Shashidhar Sharma argues that the workplace of the next decade will be shaped not by technology alone, but by the leaders and professionals who understand how to design environments in which genuine human intelligence can flourish alongside it.
This is the first volume in The Edge of Intelligence series – a body of work for the leaders, professionals, and organisations navigating the most consequential transformation of working life in a century.
